Local Roots
Built for the Fox Valley

Hallmark AI is based in Appleton, Wisconsin — and that's not incidental. The Fox Cities, Green Bay, Oshkosh, and the broader Northeast Wisconsin region have a strong economic foundation in manufacturing, healthcare, insurance, education, agriculture, and professional services. These industries stand to benefit enormously from practical AI adoption.

Most AI consultants and educators are based in coastal tech hubs, speaking a language that doesn't always translate to a main street manufacturer, a family-owned clinic, or a regional nonprofit. Hallmark AI bridges that gap — combining Silicon Valley technical depth with the pragmatism and trust that Midwest organizations expect.

Regional research* shows that the Fox Cities' average AI maturity is 2.3 out of 5. The enthusiasm is there — over half of local organizations are experimenting with AI pilots. What's missing is structured guidance, governance, workforce training, and success metrics. That's exactly where Hallmark AI works.

Approach
How we work

Every engagement starts with understanding your actual situation — not selling you a product. We meet you where you are and build from there.

01

Assess

Understand your current AI maturity, data readiness, workflows, and organizational goals.

02

Prioritize

Identify the highest-impact, lowest-risk use cases first. Not every process needs AI. We help you find the ones that do.

03

Implement

Integrate AI into real workflows with hands-on support. Build governance, train your team, and establish measurement from day one.

04

Measure

Track outcomes against defined success metrics. Refine, expand, and build organizational AI capability over time — not dependency.

About
Meet Anshuman

Anshuman is a Silicon Valley native bringing 12 years of hands-on AI engineering and product leadership to Northeast Wisconsin. He currently serves as Lead AI Engineer at HealthEdge, where he works on enterprise workflow automation, agentic platform development, and AI tooling enablement.

His work sits at the center of the current AI productivity challenge: moving beyond chatbots and text generation into agentic tools and systems embedded in real business operations.

Before HealthEdge, Anshuman shaped strategy for a major new market vertical as a Product Manager at PayPal. As a Google DeepMind Fellow, he earned a Master's in Computer Science from UC San Diego and completed his undergraduate degree at UC Berkeley with a triple major in Business Administration, Statistics, and Economics.

"The problem is no longer 'Can people use ChatGPT?' The real problem is helping people and organizations understand where AI is useful, where it is risky, and how to integrate it into workflows for actual productivity."
— Anshuman Dewangan, Founder
